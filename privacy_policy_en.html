Anonymous Shorts ("the Service", "we") is an anonymous text-based short-form content app. This Privacy Policy explains what data we process, why, for how long, and what rights you have. It is written to comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, and the Personal Information Protection Act of the Republic of Korea (PIPA).

1. Data Controller

  • Controller: Minwoo Choi (individual operator, Republic of Korea)
  • Contact: anonymous.shorts.official@gmail.com

2. Data We Process

The Service has no account system. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contact list, or advertising identifiers.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): processing your UUID, content, and vote records to provide the core features of the Service you request.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): retaining IP addresses and content archives for 90 days to prevent abuse of an anonymous service, respond to legal claims, and cooperate with lawful investigations. Given the anonymity of the Service and the short, fixed retention period, we assess that these interests are not overridden by your rights and freedoms.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): disclosure of data where required by applicable law or a valid court order.

4. Content Moderation and Automated Processing

  • All submitted text passes through a prohibited-word filter and an AI-based safety check (OpenAI Moderation API). Content flagged as harmful is automatically rejected or hidden.
  • Posts that accumulate user reports beyond a threshold are automatically hidden. Repeated violations may result in automated posting restrictions applied to the device.
  • These automated decisions affect only your ability to post within the Service. You can contest any moderation or restriction decision and request human review at any time by contacting anonymous.shorts.official@gmail.com. We respond within 15 days.

5. Sharing and International Transfers

5.1 No sale, no advertising sharing

We do not sell personal data and do not share it with advertisers or analytics providers. We use no cookies, no tracking SDKs, and no third-party analytics.

5.2 Disclosure to authorities

We disclose data only where required by applicable law or pursuant to a valid legal process (e.g., a warrant).

5.4 Server location

Our servers are operated in the Republic of Korea. For users in the EU/EEA, the European Commission has issued an adequacy decision for the Republic of Korea, so transfers to Korea are permitted under GDPR Art. 45.

6. Retention and Deletion

  • An automated server process runs every 30 minutes and permanently deletes data whose retention period has expired.
  • Deletion is performed by physically removing the database records (hard delete). Deleted data cannot be recovered.
  • Feed content: removed from the feed 24 hours after posting, then permanently deleted.
  • IP addresses and archived content: automatically deleted 90 days after creation.
  • If you use in-app deletion ([Settings > Delete My Data]), your content, credits, vote records, and restriction records are deleted immediately. The 90-day archive (including IP) is retained for the remainder of its period on the legal-defense grounds described in Section 3, then deleted.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law (GDPR Art. 15–21, PIPA, and similar laws), you have the right to:

  • Access / Portability: use [Settings > Download My Data] in the app to instantly export your posts, vote records, and credit balance in a machine-readable format.
  • Erasure: use [Settings > Delete My Data] for immediate deletion, or email us.
  • Rectification, Restriction, Objection: contact us at the address in Section 1. We respond without undue delay and within one month.
  • Lodge a complaint: with your local data protection supervisory authority (for EU/EEA users), the UK Information Commissioner's Office (for UK users), or the Personal Information Protection Commission of Korea.

Because we hold no account information, rights requests made by email may require you to provide information available in your app's [Settings] screen so we can locate the data associated with your device.

8. Children

The Service is not directed to children. You must be at least 14 years old, or older where your local law sets a higher minimum age for consenting to data processing without parental authorization (up to 16 in some EU/EEA countries). We do not knowingly process children's data; if we learn that we have, we will delete it without delay.

9. Security

  • All traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS); API requests are authenticated with HMAC-SHA256 signatures to prevent forgery.
  • Rate limiting and request-size limits mitigate abuse; administrative credentials are stored as BCrypt hashes with brute-force lockout.
  • Database credentials are isolated in environment variables; data access is limited to a single operator.

10. Changes to This Policy

We will announce changes in the app at least 7 days before they take effect (30 days for changes that materially affect your rights).
